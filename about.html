Our Beginning

Born from a Love of Coffee

"We didn't open a coffee shop. We opened a community."

Brew Haven was born in 2015 when founder Amara Chen returned from a year of travelling through Ethiopia and Colombia, inspired by the incredible coffee cultures she experienced. Armed with a vision and a vintage La Marzocco espresso machine, she opened our first location in London's Brew District.

What started as a 12-seat shop has grown into a destination for coffee lovers, remote workers, and friends seeking a warm, welcoming space. Our beans are still ethically sourced directly from partner farms and roasted on-site every single day.

Every choice we make — from the cups we use to the music we play — is designed to create moments of joy and connection.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2015

Opened First Shop

Amara opens our 12-seat original location on Coffee Lane, Brew District. A small dream, a big vision.

2017

On-site Roastery

We installed a professional roasting setup to roast our beans daily on-site — the freshest coffee in London.

2018

Won Best Local Café

Awarded 'Best Independent Café in London' by the Coffee Association of Great Britain. A milestone we cherish.

2022

Expanded Premises

We expanded to a 60-seat space with our beautiful new interior design, an outdoor terrace, and private events room.

2026

100,000 Customers Served

A landmark we celebrate with our community. Over 100,000 cups served to incredible people from all over the world.
